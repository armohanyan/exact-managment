About your instructor

Mushegh Avetisyan – trainer and coach for project management, professional development, and coaching.

Background

Mushegh is based in Yerevan, Armenia. He combines hands-on experience in project delivery, crisis management, and technology with a focus on helping individuals and teams perform better.

He holds a background in Crisis Management and Economics from the Armenian Crisis Management Academy and has worked across sectors including software development, aviation, and security. That mix of structure, risk awareness, and collaboration feeds directly into his teaching.

What he teaches

His courses cover project management (from fundamentals to Agile and Scrum), leadership and influence, and coaching for personal and professional growth. The goal is always practical: tools and mindsets you can use right away at work.

Why learn with Mushegh

  • Real-world experience in project delivery, crisis management, and team collaboration
  • Clear, structured content – no fluff, focused on what works
  • Small groups and interactive sessions so you can ask questions and practice
